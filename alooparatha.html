Aloo Paratha

Aloo paratha is hearty, rustic, and deeply satisfying. The spiced potato filling is tucked inside soft wheat dough, rolled out, and pan-fried until golden and crisp. Each bite delivers warmth, comfort, and a hint of spice—perfect with curd, pickle, or a pat of butter.

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cooking Time

10 minutes

Servings

1

Tags: All Breakfast Dessert

Instructions

Step 1

Mix flour with water and a pinch of salt. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest.

Step 2

Combine mashed potatoes with spices, chilies, and coriander.

Step 3

Divide dough into balls, flatten, fill with potato mix, seal, and roll gently.

Step 4

Roast on a hot tawa with ghee until both sides are golden brown. Serve hot.

Ingredients

2 cups whole wheat flour

3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed

1–2 green chilies, finely chopped

½ teaspoon cumin seeds

½ teaspoon garam masala

½ teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)

Salt, to taste

Fresh coriander, chopped

Ghee or oil, for cooking

0.5 Lemon

Corriander Leaves
