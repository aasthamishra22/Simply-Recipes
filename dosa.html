Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is crisp, golden, and filled with a savory potato masala. The fermented rice-lentil batter creates a delicate crunch, while the spiced filling adds depth. Served with chutneys and sambar, it’s a South Indian classic that’s both light and flavorful.

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cooking Time

15 minutes

Servings

2

Tags: All Breakfast Dessert

Instructions

Step 1

Soak rice, dal, and poha. Grind and ferment overnight.

Step 2

Sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, and chilies. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes.

Step 3

Spread batter thin on a hot tawa. Drizzle oil and cook until crisp.

Step 4

Place potato masala in center, fold, and serve with chutneys.

Ingredients

1 cup rice

¼ cup urad dal

2 tablespoons poha

3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed

1 onion, sliced

1 green chili, chopped

½ teaspoon mustard seeds

- Curry leaves

Turmeric, salt
