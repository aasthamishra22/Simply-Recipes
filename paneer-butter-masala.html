Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer butter masala is smooth, rich, and mildly spiced. Cubes of soft paneer are simmered in a buttery tomato gravy, infused with cream and aromatic spices. It’s indulgent yet balanced—a restaurant-style favorite made at home.

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cooking Time

25 minutes

Servings

2

Tags: All Breakfast Dessert

Instructions

Step 1

Cook onions, ginger-garlic, and tomato puree in butter.

Step 2

Mix in garam masala, salt, sugar, and kasuri methi.

Step 3

Gently stir in paneer cubes and simmer for 5–7 minutes.

Step 4

Add cream, stir, and serve with naan or rice.

Ingredients

200g paneer, cubed

2 tomatoes, pureed

1 onion, chopped

1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

2 tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons cream

½ teaspoon garam masala

Salt, sugar
